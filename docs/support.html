FAQ

How do I join a game?

Open the app, tap "Join a Game", and enter the 6-character code your Game Master gave you. Enter your name and you're in.

Why does the app need background location?

Your Game Master needs to see your position in real time, even when your phone is in your pocket. Without background location, tracking stops when you leave the app.

How do I stop sharing my location?

Tap "Leave" on the player screen, or simply close out of the game. You can also revoke location permissions in your device settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile (person icon on the games list) and tap "Delete My Account". This permanently removes all your data.

The app says location permission is denied

Go to your device Settings, find Outdoor GM, and set Location to "Always" (iOS) or "Allow all the time" (Android).

I'm not seeing player locations as a GM

Make sure your players have joined the game, granted location permissions, and have an internet connection. Player dots appear within 30 seconds of joining.
