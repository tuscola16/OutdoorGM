Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Outdoor GM ("the App") is a real-time location-sharing game for iOS and Android. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — used for account authentication and password resets. We do not send marketing emails or share your address with third parties.
  • Display name — the name you choose when joining a game, visible to your Game Master and other participants.
  • Precise GPS location — collected continuously (foreground and background) while you are actively participating in a game. Location is shared in real time with the Game Master(s) of your current game session only.
  • Device push token — used to deliver push notifications (e.g., checkpoint arrival alerts). The token is stored in our database and associated with your game membership.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication — your email and password are used to sign in to your account. Password reset emails are sent to your address on request.
  • Gameplay — your location is displayed on the Game Master's map and checked against checkpoint geofences to trigger arrival alerts.
  • Notifications — push notifications inform Game Masters of checkpoint arrivals.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging) — processes and stores your data. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps SDK — renders maps in the app. Google may collect anonymized usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Expo / EAS — build and update infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

  • Location data is retained only while a game is active. When a game ends, location tracking stops.
  • Game records (arrivals, checkpoints, membership) are retained until the game creator deletes them or you delete your account.
  • You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. This removes all your data from our systems, including your user profile, game memberships, and location history.

5. Data Sharing

Your location and display name are shared only with Game Master(s) in your active game session. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — you can view your data in the app (profile, game history).
  • Deletion — use the "Delete My Account" button on the Profile screen, or contact us at the email below.
  • Withdraw consent — revoke location permissions in your device settings at any time. The app will stop tracking but you will not be able to participate in games.

7. Children

Outdoor GM is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over TLS (HTTPS). Firebase enforces security rules that restrict data access to authenticated, authorized users only.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Effective date" at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
